**Nocturne**

* A demonic amalgamation drawn from the nightmares that haunt every sentient mind, Nocturne has become a primordial force of pure evil.
* It is liquidly chaotic in aspect, a faceless shadow with cold eyes and armed with wicked-looking blades.
* After freeing itself from the spirit realm, Nocturne descended upon the waking world, to feed upon the kind of terror that can only thrive in true darkness.

**Gameplay:**

Nocturne is an assassin and fighter champion in League of Legends. He is primarily played in the jungle role and uses mana as his resource. His range type is melee, and his adaptive type is physical.
