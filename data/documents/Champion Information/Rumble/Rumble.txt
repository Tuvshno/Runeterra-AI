### Base Statistics

**Rumble**

* Health: 625 (+105)
* Health regeneration (per 5s): 7 (+0.6)
* Resource: N/A
* Armor: 36 (+4.7)
* Attack damage: 64 (+3.2)
* Magic resist: 28 (+1.55)
* Crit. damage: 175%
* Move. speed: 345
* Attack range: 125
* Attack speed: Base AS: 0.644

---

### Abilities

**Junkyard Titan**

* Innate: Rumble's basic ability casts generate Heat, which refreshes on subsequent casts and decays by 10 Heat per second after not using any basic ability within 4 seconds or The Equalizer within 2 seconds.
* Rumble's mech enters the Danger Zone when at or above 50 Heat, and becomes Overheated while at 150 Heat.

**Danger Zone:**

* Rumble's mech enters the Danger Zone, empowering his basic abilities.

**Overheated:**

* Rumble's mech becomes Overheated, disabling his abilities as his Heat decays back down to 0 over 4 seconds. During this time, he gains 50% − 130% (based on level) bonus attack speed and empowers his basic attacks to deal 5 − 40 (based on level) (± 25% AP) (± 6% of the target's maximum health) bonus magic damage on-hit. The damage based on the target's health ratio is capped at 65 − 300 (based on level) against monsters.

---

#### Junkyard Titan - Ability Details

* **Heat Generation:** Rumble gains 20 Heat when using a basic ability or when The Equalizer is activated. He can store up to 2 charges of The Equalizer.

* **Danger Zone:** Rumble's mech enters the Danger Zone when his Heat is at or above 50. While in the Danger Zone, Rumble's abilities are empowered.

* **Overheated:** Rumble's mech becomes Overheated when his Heat is at or above 150. While Overheated, Rumble cannot use his abilities, but he gains bonus attack speed and his basic attacks deal bonus magic damage on-hit.

---

#### Junkyard Titan - Ability Order

1. Basic Attack
2. The Equalizer
3. Flamespitter
4. Scrap Shield
5. Electro Harpoon

### Flamespitter

* Cooldown: 10 / 9 / 8 / 7 / 6 seconds
* Cast Time: None / 0.5833 seconds
* Range: 600 - 50 units (front cone)
* Angle: 64°
* Active: Rumble generates 20 Heat to activate his flamethrower for 3 seconds, dealing magic damage every 0.25 seconds to enemies hit in a frontal cone, reduced against minions.
* Total Magic Damage: 125 / 140 / 155 / 170 / 185 (± 110% AP) (± 6 / 7 / 8 / 9 / 10% of target's maximum health)
* Magic Damage per Tick: 10.417 / 11.667 / 12.917 / 14.167 / 15.417 (± 9.17% AP) (± 0.5 / 0.583 / 0.667 / 0.75 / 0.833% of target's maximum health)
* Modified Minion Damage: 55 / 60 / 65 / 70 / 75%

---

### Scrap Shield

* Cooldown: 6 seconds at all ranks
* Cast Time: None
* Active: Rumble generates 20 Heat to grant himself a shield for 1.5 seconds.
* Shield Strength: 25 / 55 / 85 / 115 / 145 (± 25% AP) (± 4% of maximum health)
* Rumble also gains bonus movement speed for 1 to 1.32 seconds.
* Enhanced Shield Strength: 37.5 / 82.5 / 127.5 / 172.5 / 217.5 (± 37.5% AP) (± 6% of maximum health)
* Enhanced Bonus Movement Speed: 15 / 22.5 / 30 / 37.5 / 45%

---

### Electro Harpoon

* Cooldown: 0.5 seconds
* Cast Time: 0.25 seconds
* Recharge: 6 seconds
* Range: 950 / 890 units
* Width: 120 units
* Speed: 2000
* Active: Rumble generates 20 Heat to shoot a harpoon in the target direction that deals magic damage to the first enemy hit and inflicts them with magic resistance reduction for 4 seconds and slows them for 2 seconds. These effects stack additively with multiple harpoons, refreshing their duration and stacking up to 2 times.
* Magic Damage: 60 / 85 / 110 / 135 / 160 (± 50% AP)
* Total Magic Damage: 120 / 170 / 220 / 270 / 320 (± 100% AP)
* Magic Resistance Reduction: 10 / 12 / 14 / 16 / 18%
* Total Magic Resistance Reduction: 20 / 24 / 28 / 32 / 36%
* Slow: 15 / 20 / 25 / 30 / 35%
* Total Slow: 30 / 40 / 50 / 60 / 70%

---

#### Electro Harpoon - Cooldown Reduction

Rumble's cooldown reduction does not apply to Electro Harpoon.

---

### The Equalizer

* Cooldown: 130 / 105 / 80 seconds
* Cast Time: None / 0.5833 seconds
* Target Range: 1700 units
* Range: -175 / 1135 units (behind target)
* Width: 410 - 368.4 units
* Speed: 1600
* Active: Rumble deploys a barrage of rockets along the target path that land in a line over 0.75 seconds, creating a field of fire for 4.5 seconds after the last rocket lands.
* Enemies struck by the impact or within the field are marked as Burning for 1 second, which deals magic damage every 0.5 seconds, slows by 35%, and refreshes continuously while in the area.
* Magic Damage per Tick: 70 / 105 / 140 (± 17.5% AP)
* Minimum Magic Damage: 140 / 210 / 280 (± 35% AP)
* Maximum Magic Damage: 700 / 1050 / 1400 (± 175% AP)

---

#### The Equalizer - Map-Specific Differences

* The Equalizer deals full damage to structures.
* The Equalizer's rockets no longer stack slow.
* The Equalizer's slow duration is reduced to 1.5 seconds.
* The Equalizer's area of effect no longer extends to champions outside of its intended range.

---

#### The Equalizer - Ultra Rapid Fire Differences

* The Equalizer no longer disables Rumble's abilities.
* The Equalizer no longer grants bonus damage or magic resistance reduction while in the Danger Zone.
