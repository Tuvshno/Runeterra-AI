Gameplay:
Lillia is a Skirmisher, Legacy, Fighter, and Mage champion in League of Legends. She is a Jungle champion and uses Mana as her resource. Her range type is melee, and her adaptive type is magic. Her store price is 4800, and 880 is required to craft her. Lillia's base statistics include 605 health, 410 mana, 2.5 health regeneration per second, 11.5 mana regeneration per second, 22 armor, 32 magic resist, 175% crit damage, and 330 move speed. Her attack range is 325, and her attack speed is 0.625 with a base attack damage of 0. Her attack windup is 14.706%, and her attack speed ratio is N/A. She has a unit radius of 65, a selection radius of 135, a pathing radius of 35, and an acquirement radius of 400.

Lillia's special statistics include ARAM, Nexus Blitz, One For All, URF, Ult Spellbook, Arena, Damage Dealt, and Damage Received. Her Damage Dealt statistics show that she deals -5% Damage Dealt and receives +5% Damage Received against champions, -2% Damage Dealt and +0% Damage Received against monsters, and 0% Damage Dealt and 0% Damage Received against turrets.

Lillia has three abilities: Dream-Laden Bough, Blooming Blows, and Watch Out! Eep!

Dream-Laden Bough:
Lillia's abilities apply Dream Dust, dealing 5% of the target's maximum health as magic damage over 3 seconds, capped at 70 - 150 (based on level) against monsters. Lillia heals for 39 - 54 (based on level) (+ 15% AP) against large monsters and 6 - 90 (based on level) (+ 30% AP) against champions over the duration of Dream Dust, reduced to 33% effectiveness for each target beyond the first. She may only heal against 1 monster at a time.

Blooming Blows (Q):
Cost: 65 Mana
Cooldown: 6 / 5.5 / 5 / 4.5 / 4 seconds
Cast time: 0.25 seconds
Effect radius: 225 / 485
Passive: Lillia gains a Prance stack for 5 seconds whenever she hits at least one enemy with her abilities, refreshing up to 4 stacks. Each stack grants Lillia 3% / 4% / 5% / 6% / 7% (+ 1% per 100 AP) bonus movement speed.
Active: Lillia swings her censer around her, dealing 35 / 50 / 65 / 80 / 95 (+ 35% AP) magic damage to nearby enemies. Enemies hit within the outer edge of the area additionally take the same amount as bonus true damage.

Watch Out! Eep! (W):
Cost: 50 Mana
Cooldown: 14 / 13 / 12 / 11 / 10 seconds
Cast time: 0.25 seconds
Target range: 500
Effect radius: 65 / 250
Active: Lillia rushes to 150 units in front of the target area over 0.75 seconds, though not through terrain, and winds up for a strike, dealing 70 / 85 / 100 / 115 / 130 (+ 30% AP) magic damage in an area. Enemies hit within the center are dealt 200% increased damage. Watch Out! Eep! deals 50% damage to minions.

Swirlseed (E):
Cost: 70 Mana
Cooldown: 12 seconds
Cast time: 0.4 seconds
Target range: 700 / Global
Width: 120
Effect radius: 150
Active: Lillia lodges a seed at the target area that rolls forward indefinitely. The seed detonates upon colliding with an enemy or terrain while on the ground, dealing 70 / 90 / 110 / 130 / 150 (+ 40% AP) magic damage to enemies in a cone, slowing them by 40% for 3 seconds and revealing them for 2 seconds. Swirlseed will cast at max range if cast beyond that. The spot at which the seed will collide with terrain is highlighted to Lillia while it is rolling.

Map-Specific Differences:
Lillia's abilities have some differences when used on specific maps. For example, on the Howling Abyss map, the cooldown for Swirlseed is increased to 18 seconds.
