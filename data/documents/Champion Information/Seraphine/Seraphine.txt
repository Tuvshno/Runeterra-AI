**Abilities**

[Stage Presence]

* Effect radius: 800
* Speed: 3000
* Innate - Echo:
  * Seraphine's basic abilities generate a stack of Echo, stacking up to 2 times.
  * At 2 stacks, Seraphine's next basic ability casts an additional time for 1 mana after a 0.033 seconds delay, consuming all Echo stacks after the cast time of the second cast completes.
  * Seraphine gains full stacks of Echo when the game starts and upon respawning.
* Innate - Harmony:
  * Seraphine's abilities grant a Note to herself and all nearby allied champions for 6 seconds, up to 4 Notes at a time per unit.
  * Seraphine's next basic attack gains 25 bonus attack range per active Note and fires all of them at the target, dealing 5 - 20 (based on level) (+ 4% AP) magic damage for each Note discharged.
  * Subsequent Notes deal 95% of the previous Note's damage to non-minions.
  * Notes deal 200% bonus damage to minions.

[High Note (Q)]

* Target range: 900
* Effect radius: 350
* Effect speed: 1200
* Cast time: 0.25 seconds
* Active: Seraphine hurls a soundwave to the target location that quickly expands in a radius upon impact, dealing 55 / 65 / 75 / 85 / 95 (+ 55% AP) magic damage to enemies in the area, increased by 0% - 50% (based on target's missing health).
* Mana cost: 65 / 70 / 75 / 80 / 85
* Cooldown: 10 / 8.75 / 7.5 / 6.5 / 5.5 seconds

[Surround Sound (W)]

* Effect radius: 800
* Cast time: 0.25 seconds
* Active: Seraphine inspires nearby ally champions with her music, granting herself 20% (+ 4% per 100 AP) decaying bonus movement speed and her allies 8% (+ 1.6% per 100 AP) bonus movement speed. She and her allies are also shielded for 60 - 120 (based on level) (+ 30% AP) for 2.5 seconds.
* Surround Sound will heal Seraphine and all nearby allied champions for 5% (+ 1.125% per 100 AP) of missing health per ally after a 2.5-second delay if Seraphine already had a shield at the time of cast.
* Surround Sound's effects can stack up to 2 times.
* Mana cost: 50 / 70 / 90 / 110 / 130
* Cooldown: 26 / 25 / 24 / 23 / 22 seconds

[Beat Drop (E)]

* Target range: 1300
* Width: 140
* Speed: 1200
* Cast time: 0.25 seconds
* Active: Seraphine fires a heavy soundwave in a line in the target direction, dealing 60 / 85 / 110 / 135 / 160 (+ 35% AP) magic damage to enemies struck, reduced to 60 / 70 / 80 / 90 / 100% against minions, and slowing them by 99% for 1 second.
* Enemies that are already slowed are also rooted for the same duration. Enemies that are immobilized or grounded are also stunned for the same duration.
* Mana cost: 60 / 70 / 80 / 90 / 100
* Cooldown: 13 / 12 / 11 / 10 / 9 seconds

[Encore (R)]

* Target range: 1200
* Width: 320
* Speed: 1600
* Cast time: 0.5 seconds
* Active: Seraphine projects a captivating force in the target direction that deals 150 / 200 / 250 (+ 60% AP) magic damage to all enemies struck and charms, slows, and reveals them for 1.25 / 1.5 / 1.75 seconds.
* Encore's projectile resets its remaining travel distance whenever it strikes an allied or enemy champion, excluding Seraphine. Additionally, all struck allied champions gain 4 Notes.
* Mana cost: 100
* Cooldown: 160 / 140 / 120 seconds
