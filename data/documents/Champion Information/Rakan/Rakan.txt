        **Gameplay**
          * Class(es): Catcher, Legacy, Support
          * Position(s): Support
          * Resource: Mana
          * Range type: Melee
          * Adaptive type: Magic
          * Store price: 4800 (or 880 RP)
          * Crafting cost: +2880 RP
          * Ratings: Damage: 1, Toughness: 2, Control: 3, Mobility: 3, Utility: 3
          * Difficulty: [Medium to High]
          
        **Abilities**
          * Fey Feathers (Passive): Rakan gains a shield when not in combat and reduces Fey Feathers' cooldown with each enemy champion hit.
          * Lover's Leap (Passive): Rakan and Xayah can Recall together, with both champions reaching base at the same time.
          
          * Fey Feathers (Q): Rakan gains a shield based on level and AP. Damaging an enemy champion reduces Fey Feathers' cooldown.
          * Gleaming Quill (Q): Rakan throws an enchanted feather dealing magic damage to the first enemy hit. If the target is a champion or epic monster, Rakan and nearby allies are healed when an allied champion enters the marked area.
          * Cooldown: 11 / 10 / 9 / 8 / 7 seconds
          * Mana cost: 45
          
          * Grand Entrance (W): Rakan dashes to a location, dealing magic damage and knocking up enemies in a radius. Rakan is knocked down during Grand Entrance.
          * Cooldown: 16 / 14.5 / 13 / 11.5 / 10 seconds
          * Mana cost: 50 / 60 / 70 / 80 / 90
          
          * Battle Dance (E): Rakan leaps to an ally champion, shielding them and granting him bonus movement speed. Battle Dance can be recast for free within 5 seconds.
          * Cooldown: 20 / 18 / 16 / 14 / 12 seconds
          * Mana cost: 60 / 70 / 80 / 90 / 100
          
          * The Quickness (R): Rakan gains a buff, increasing his movement speed and dealing magic damage to enemies he collides with, charming them for a duration.
          * Cooldown: 130 / 110 / 90 seconds
          * Mana cost: 100

        **Trivia**
          * Rakan was the first dual release alongside Xayah in 2017.
          * Rakan's cloak is actually his wings disguised by Vastayan magic.
          * Rakan and Xayah are sometimes depicted together with combined wings.
          * Rakan has the second highest basic attack range amongst melee champions (300 units).
          * Rakan's favorite human food is chocolate.
          * Rakan's dance references Jailhouse Rock by Elvis Presley.
          * Rakan deals 1 less damage with Melee Attack.
          * Rakan's name resembles the Malay noun 'rakan', which means 'friend, relative'.
          * Rakan is the only champion with 3 points at 3 skills by the client classification.
