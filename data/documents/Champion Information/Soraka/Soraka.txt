**Abilities**

- [**Salvation**] (Passive)
  - Soraka gains 90% bonus movement speed while facing nearby allied champions below 40% of their maximum health.

- [**Starcall**] (Active)
  - Cost: 45 / 50 / 55 / 60 / 65 Mana
  - Cooldown: 8 / 7 / 6 / 5 / 4
  - Cast time: 0.25s
  - Target range: 800
  - Effect radius: 265
  - Soraka calls down a star upon the target location. The star grants sight of the area before landing and deals magic damage to enemies hit and slows them by 30% for 1.5 seconds. If Starcall hits at least one enemy champion, the star dust returns to Soraka to grant her Rejuvenation for 2.5 seconds. While Soraka has Rejuvenation, Astral Infusion will also grant the effects of Rejuvenation to the target ally for the same duration.
  - Magic Damage: 85 / 120 / 155 / 190 / 225 (+ 35% AP)

- [**Astral Infusion**] (Active)
  - Cost: 10% of Soraka's maximum health + 40 / 45 / 50 / 55 / 60 Mana
  - Cooldown: 6 / 5 / 4 / 3 / 2
  - Cast time: 0.25s
  - Target range: 550
  - Soraka heals the target allied champion.
  - Heal: 90 / 110 / 130 / 150 / 170 (+ 50% AP)
  - If cast while under Rejuvenation, the health cost will be reduced by a percentage.
  - Health Cost Reduction: 80 / 85 / 90 / 95 / 100%
  - Reduced Health Cost: 2 / 1.5 / 1 / 0.5 / 0% of maximum health

**Equinox** (Active)

- Cost: 70 / 75 / 80 / 85 / 90 Mana
- Cooldown: 20 / 19 / 18 / 17 / 16
- Cast time: 0.25s
- Target range: 925
- Effect radius: 260
- Soraka creates a celestial zone at the target location that deals magic damage to enemy champions within at the time of cast. The zone then persists for 1.5 seconds and silences enemies within. Afterwards, the zone erupts to deal the same damage to enemy champions within and root them for a duration.
- Magic Damage: 70 / 95 / 120 / 145 / 170 (+ 40% AP)
- Total Magic Damage: 140 / 190 / 240 / 290 / 340 (+ 80% AP)
- Root Duration: 1 / 1.25 / 1.5 / 1.75 / 2s

**Wish** (Active)

- Cost: 100 Mana
- Cooldown: 150 / 135 / 120
- Cast time: 0.25s
- Effect radius: Global
- Soraka calls upon the stars, healing herself and all allied champions, increased by 50% on targets below 40% of their maximum health.
- Heal: 150 / 250 / 350 (+ 50% AP)
- Increased Heal: 225 / 375 / 525 (+ 75% AP)
