**Abilities**

* Deathbringer Stance
	+ Static cooldown: 22 − 10 (based on level) seconds
	+ Periodically empowers next basic attack to gain bonus range and deal bonus magic damage equal to 4% − 12% (based on level) of target's maximum health
	+ Heals for 80% of post-mitigation bonus damage dealt, reduced to 25% against minions
	+ Cooldown reduced when hitting at least one enemy champion or large monster with a basic attack or an ability
* The Darkin Blade
	+ Three-part ability
	+ First cast: 625 × 180-unit rectangular area, with Aatrox centered on the back line and the Sweetspot at the farthest edge
	+ Second cast: trapezoidal area, with the Sweetspot at the farthest edge and the hitbox beginning 100 units behind Aatrox
	+ Third cast: 300-radius circular area centered on a target location that is 200 units in front of Aatrox, with a 180-radius Sweetspot within
	+ Deals 55 / 60 / 65 / 70% (based on level) damage against minions and knocks up for 0.5 seconds longer against monsters
	+ Maximum non-minion damage: 60 / 150 / 240 / 330 / 420 (+360 / 405 / 450 / 495 / 540% AD)
